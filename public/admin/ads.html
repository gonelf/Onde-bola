📢 Manage ads
Add a unit, pick where it should show, and Save. For a "key + size" iframe banner (e.g. highperformanceformat.com), switch the unit to Banner ad and fill in just the key and size — the script is generated for you, so there's nothing to paste wrong. For anything else, leave it as Custom snippet and paste the full <script>…</script> the network gives you. Use Preview to render a unit in a sandbox. Stored server-side (needs ADMIN_USER / ADMIN_PASSWORD and KV configured); live within a few minutes. Self-placing formats (popunder / social bar) won't show in Preview, and ad blockers suppress previews — disable shields to preview real ads.
